Personal Projects and Other Activities

Robot Arm Project - January 2015 - Present

An ongoing project, I am working with Ethan Glassman to construct a modular robotic arm. The target payload is 1 lb, and the target reach is 2 ft. We hope to create this to serve as a modular, robust hardware platform. Funded in part by the generous financial support of the student chapter of ASME at Washington University.

Lightweight Aerial Filming Quadcopter - May 2014-Present

This quadcopter design is unique and ambitious. The arms of the quadcopter are three-member trusses, using Spectra line as a high-rigidity tensile member. This enables weight savings of 50% over comparable 450mm frames.

Specifications:

  • Total Mass: 700 grams (Frame only: 200 grams)
  • Total Thrust: 27.5N (6.2lbs)
  • Maximum Payload: 700 grams
  • Expected Endurance: 15 minutes
