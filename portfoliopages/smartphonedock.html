Custom Smartphone Holder Project

The initial problem to solve. With the advent of high-end GPS software available for smartphones, it no longer made sense to have a stand-alone GPS unit. But the cellphone needed a solid mount to be safe and easy to use.

Solidworks CAD for first hardware revision, built around a 3D model of a Galaxy Nexus smartphone to mount into a Saab 9-3.

Due to the complex shape of the Galaxy Nexus smartphone, it was necessary to use some fancy manufacture techniques, fortunately, I have a 3-axis CNC mill running off Solidworks and it was relatively straightforward to machine an initial version.

From here, it was straightforward to assemble, install, and use this cell phone mount! It works great, there is now a safe place to put my cell phone when I am using it for a GPS unit.
