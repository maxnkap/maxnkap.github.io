Sunglasses Clip Project

In summer of 2013 I had a pair of sunglasses using interchangeable lenses, but it was nearly impossible to get new nose clips without a full pair of lenses. I liked the frames, but I wanted to be able to put in safety glasses lenses on occasion when working in a machine shop. I decided to try CADding my own, and making a custom lense.

First, I built a CAD model. One of the considerations I tried to make was manufacture. As the parts were very small, I decided it made more sense to get a 3D print service to print parts for me. This meant that threaded inserts made the most sense as most of Shapeway's materials are hard to thread.

Once the parts arrived, I test assembled one clip. See it here compared with the original.
