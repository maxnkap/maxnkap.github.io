Mahe Medical GmbH & Co KG - Spring 2011

     By the time I completed my other internship positions, I was well prepared to serve as a CNC machine operator at Mahe. I had full responsibility for a number of different machines over the course of my internship at Mahe, including Swiss lathes and 3-axis milling centers. I also spent time working in the engineering office and the quality control department.

Weber Instrumente GmbH - Spring 2011

     At Weber, I had a flexible assignment in the factory. I assisted where I was needed in the production of Weber's specialty orthopedic instruments. This took me from manufacturing parts in the shop, to working with CAD and PDM software alongside design engineers.

Karl Leibinger Medizintechnik of the KLS Martin Group - Winter 2011

     Karl Leibinger is a designer and manufacturer of a diverse array of medical devices, from operating room lighting to orthopedic instruments, to bio-resorbable implants.
I was enrolled as a Praktikant in the formal training program at Leibinger. During my time there, I received training in a diverse number of manufacturing and assembly techniques, and did design work in the engineering office.

AAP Implantate GmbH - Fall 2010

     I served as an assistant technician at the AAP factory, maintaining CNC machines and learning general shop practices. I helped manufacture bone plates & screws, and a number of different orthopedic implants, primarily knee and hip implants.
